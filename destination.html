Swat, Pakistan:

Places to Visit:

  • White Palace
  • Mahodand Lake
  • Malam Jabba
  • Miandam
  • Ushu Forest

Things to Do:

  • Go white water rafting on the Swat River.
  • Visit the Takht-i-Bahi, a Buddhist monastery that dates back to the 1st century AD.
  • Visit the local markets to buy fresh produce, handicrafts, and other souvenirs.
  • Try the local cuisine, such as chapli kebabs, korma, and naan.

Local Tips:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Be aware of your surroundings and take precautions to stay safe.
  • Learn a few basic Pashto phrases, such as "hello," "goodbye," "thank you," and "please."
  • Learn about the history and culture of Swat before you go.
  • Support the local economy by shopping at local businesses and eating at local restaurants.
